Common Emergency Scenarios We Handle

My semi truck won't start on I-565 - can you help?
Absolutely! We handle roadside breakdowns 24/7. Common causes include dead batteries, starter problems, fuel system issues, or computer failures. Our mobile units carry jump packs, diagnostic scanners, and replacement parts to get you moving fast. We'll have you back on the road typically within 1-2 hours.
Construction equipment broke down at job site - how fast can you get here?
Emergency response time averages 30-45 minutes in the Huntsville metro area. We service all heavy equipment brands: Caterpillar, John Deere, Komatsu, Case, Kubota, and more. Our trucks carry hydraulic hoses, filters, pumps, and welding equipment for on-site repairs.
Diesel engine is overheating - is this an emergency?
Yes! Stop driving immediately to prevent catastrophic engine damage. Overheating can be caused by cooling system leaks, thermostat failure, or blocked radiators. Our mobile techs carry coolant, hoses, thermostats, and can perform emergency welding for cracked components.
Hydraulic system failure on my excavator - can you fix it on-site?
Most hydraulic issues can be repaired on-site. We stock hydraulic pumps, cylinders, hoses, fittings, and filters. Common problems include blown seals, contaminated fluid, or damaged hoses. Our certified hydraulic specialists can diagnose and repair most issues without towing.
Do you work on weekends and holidays?
Yes! We provide 24/7/365 emergency service. Breakdowns don't wait for business hours, and neither do we. Same expert service and response time whether it's 2 AM Sunday or Christmas Day. No extra charges for after-hours emergency calls.
What if you can't fix it on-site?
While we fix 85% of breakdowns on-site, some issues require shop-level repairs. We'll provide honest diagnosis, emergency temporary fixes to get you to safety, and arrange professional towing to our certified repair facility. No surprises - you'll know all options and costs upfront.
